About Me

Hi, I'm Charlotte. I'm currently pursuing a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Olin College of Engineering, a small and unique institution in Massachusetts.

Outside of my classes, I do satellite research with the Olin Satellite and Spectrum Technology and Policy (OSSTP) group with the vision of creating a safe and sustainable space environment for generations to come. I am currently project lead for an antenna control unit and am a part of the team researching equivalent power-flux density (EPFD) software. I am also an Academic Resource Co-Designer and course assistant for the Software Design class at Olin. In the past, I've also worked as an Electrical Engineering Intern for SENER Aerospace.

In my free time, I can be found taking photos, getting lost in my favourite book, working on my Japanese, or practicing with the Olin Fire Arts Club.
